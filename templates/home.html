Word Hunt

90-second head-to-head tournament

Play As Player 1 Play As Player 2 Spectator View

Quick Rules

  • Build words by swiping adjacent tiles (including diagonals).
  • No tile can be reused inside a single word.
  • Each player gets 90 seconds on the same board.
  • Points by word length: 3-4=1, 5=2, 6=3, 7=5, 8+=11.
